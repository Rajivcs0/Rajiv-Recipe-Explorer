Directions

Mix yogurt, tandoori masala, red chili powder, cumin powder, coriander powder, turmeric, garam masala, lemon juice, ginger-garlic paste, and salt in a bowl. Coat the chicken pieces with this marinade, cover, and refrigerate for 4-6 hours or overnight for best results.

Preheat your oven to 400¬∞F (200¬∞C) or heat your grill.

Place the marinated chicken on a baking tray or grill rack. Bake in the oven for 25-30 minutes or grill for 15-20 minutes, turning occasionally until cooked through and slightly charred.
