Directions

Heat oil in a pan and add cumin seeds. Once they splutter, add chopped onions and saut√© until golden. Add ginger-garlic paste and chopped green chilies, cook for 1-2 minutes.

Add chopped tomatoes, turmeric, coriander powder, chili powder, and salt. Cook until the tomatoes soften and oil starts to separate.

Add the chickpeas (drained and rinsed if using canned) along with some water or broth. Stir well, bring to a simmer, and cook for 10-15 minutes.

Add garam masala and fresh cilantro. Stir, and serve with rice or roti.
